# Additional Game Screens - Official Forum Documentation

## Kingdom Screen

**Source**: https://www.monarchygame.com/boards/viewtopic.php?f=116&t=1746

### Overview
General summary of your kingdom - the hub of your realm.

### Information Displayed
- **Military**: Army status and composition
- **Economy**: Income, expenses, treasury
- **Networth Score**: Overall kingdom value
- **Standing**: Position within game
- **Guild Affiliation**: Guild membership status
- **Turns**: Accumulated and available turns
- **NPP Status**: Turns left in New Player Protection
- **Crucial Statistics**: Various kingdom metrics

### News Feed
Displayed at bottom of screen:
- Infiltration attempts on your kingdom
- Sabotage attempts
- Attacks made upon your kingdom
- Money received from allies

### Quick Reference
Most other screens contain income, expenses, and treasury, but Kingdom screen provides quick general reference for entire realm.

### Banners
- **Guild Banner**: Displayed if you belong to guild (contact GM for details)
- **Personal Banner**: Displayed if enabled

### Alerts
- **Unread Messages**: Alert at top if unread message in Scribe
- Clicking alert takes you to Scribe screen

## Alliances Screen

**Source**: https://www.monarchygame.com/boards/viewtopic.php?f=116&t=1734

### Overview
Manage alliances with 3 other kingdoms, send gold to allies, or declare war or peace.

### Alliance Mechanics

#### Offering Alliance
1. Enter kingdom number in 'send envoys to' box
2. Press 'send'
3. Offer appears in target kingdom's Scribe screen
4. Target can reply, accept, or decline
5. **Limit**: Each kingdom may have up to 3 allies at any time

#### Requirements
- Must be same faith
- Available in all games except Solo mode

### Military Support

#### Support Levels (Increasing Magnitude)
1. **Patrols**: Send patrols to allies
2. **Vanguard Supplement**: Supplement their vanguard
3. **Army Dispatch**: Despatch army to march alongside their troops

#### Support Types
- **Offensive Support**: Aid ally's attacks
- **Defensive Support**: Aid ally's defense

#### Important Notes
- Supporting ally does **not** diminish your own defenses
- Your forces **may suffer losses** while aiding ally

### Financial Support

#### Sending Aid
1. Select ally from 'send aid' dropdown
2. Use 'amount' box to select portion of treasury
3. Transfer is **instant**

#### Limitations
- **Frequency**: Each kingdom may only send **one shipment per 24 hours**
- **Risk**: Money may be intercepted if enemy has set 'intercept caravan' (Thievery screen)

### War Declaration
Screen allows declaring war on enemies (details in War screen documentation).

## Bounty Screen

**Source**: https://www.monarchygame.com/boards/viewtopic.php?f=116&t=1733

### Overview
Claim rewards for killing enemy kingdoms.

### Mechanics
*(Note: Forum page contains only title - detailed mechanics available in strategy-guides/bounties.md)*

**Key Features from Strategy Guides:**
- **Land Acquisition**: 30% of killed realm's land
- **Structure Bonus**: ~20% built ratio included
- **Turn Savings**: 100+ turns equivalent
- **Strategic Timing**: Optimal after tithing exhaustion (11k-15k acres)
