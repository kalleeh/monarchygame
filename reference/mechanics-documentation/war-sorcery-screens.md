# War & Sorcery Screens - Official Forum Documentation

## War Screen

**Source**: https://www.monarchygame.com/boards/viewtopic.php?f=116&t=1738

### Overview
Selection of offensive and defensive military options.

### Turn Costs
- **Base Cost**: 4 turns per attack
- **Networth Scaling**: Cost increases if target's networth is markedly larger or smaller than your own

### Attack Types

#### 1. Controlled Strike
- Pits entirety of your force against a **fraction** of their armies
- More likely to succeed and take land
- Higher acreage aimed for = higher proportion of enemy army encountered
- Must enter "acreage to seize" amount

#### 2. Ambush (Defensive)
- Increases potence of defense for **next incoming attack only**
- Must be manually set again after triggered
- Always costs 4 turns

#### 3. Guerilla Raid
- Sends T1 and T2 troops + thieves against enemy equivalent forces
- Kills troops involved in battle
- Kills enemy peasants
- **No land ever taken** in guerilla raids

#### 4. Mob Assault
- Pits entire army **and peasantry** against enemy defenses
- Makes offensive more potent
- **Risk**: Harms economy if peasants killed
- Seizes **less land** than successful full attack

#### 5. Full Attack
- Your entire army against theirs in pitched battle
- Takes **more land than any other method** if successful

### War Declaration
After **three offensive actions** against a target:
- Populace may refuse further aggression
- Must declare official state of war via Alliances screen

## Sorcery Screen

**Source**: https://www.monarchygame.com/boards/viewtopic.php?f=116&t=1737

### Overview
Selection of offensive and defensive spells.

### Elan System
- **Fuel**: Sorcery powered by 'elan'
- **Generation**: Accrue each turn when you have enough temples
- **Scaling**: More temples = more elan per turn

### Temple Requirements
Building more temples enables access to more spells (up to 6 total).

### Backlash Mechanic
- Occurs occasionally when casting offensive spells
- **Effects**:
  - Destroys temples
  - Costs elan
  - Costs turns
- Natural danger of wielding powerful magics

### Spell List (Faith-Specific Names)

#### 1. Calming Chant
- **Availability**: All kingdoms (first spell)
- **Cost**: 2 turns, 0 elan
- **Effect**: Temporary income increase
- **Bonus**: Grants 1 elan per casting
- **Repeat**: Can cast multiple times (10 repeats = 20 turns)

#### 2. Awakened Spirits
- **Cost**: 10 elan, 2 turns
- **Effect**: Summons ghostly T1 soldiers
- **Duration**: One defensive or offensive action, then disappear
- **Quality**: All troops are 'green' (level 1)
- **Scaling**: Larger kingdom = more troops summoned
- **Limitation**: Does not benefit allies

#### 3. Shattering Calm (Offensive)
- **Cost**: 20 elan, 2 turns
- **Targets**: Enemy peasants and temples
- **Power**: More effective with considerably more temples than opponent

#### 4. Wild Design (Defensive)
- **Cost**: 40 elan, 2 turns
- **Effect**: Blocks single incoming Shattering Calm or Banshee Deluge
- **Stacking**: Can stack multiple Wild Designs simultaneously

#### 5. Blazing Noise (Fog)
- **Cost**: 60 elan, 2 turns
- **Effect**: Random chance to deflect enemy war and steal actions
- **Duration**: 24 hours before fading
- **Common Name**: "Fog"

#### 6. Banshee Deluge (Most Powerful Offensive)
- **Cost**: 80 elan, 2 turns
- **Target**: Entirety of enemy township
- **Power**: Capable of devastation with considerably more temples than opponent
- **Expense**: Most expensive spell available

### Strategic Notes
- Spell names vary by faith but gained/displayed in same order
- Temple count primary factor in offensive spell effectiveness
- Defensive spells provide protection against specific offensive spells
