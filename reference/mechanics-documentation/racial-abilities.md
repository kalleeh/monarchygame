# Racial Special Abilities - Complete Technical Specifications

**Source**: NPAC Elite Forum Analysis - "What about special racial abilities?" and race analysis guides

## Exact Special Ability Mechanics

### Human - Caravan Frequency Bonus
- **Mechanic**: Can send caravans twice as often as other races
- **Standard Cooldown**: 24 hours between caravan sends
- **Human Cooldown**: 12 hours between caravan sends
- **Strategic Value**: Bypasses sabotage attempts, enables rapid resource distribution
- **War Application**: Critical for cash distribution during coordinated strikes

### Elven - Remote Fog Casting
- **Mechanic**: Can cast fog on any guild member regardless of distance
- **Standard Limitation**: Fog only affects caster's own realm
- **Elven Advantage**: Guild-wide fog coverage from single caster
- **Strategic Value**: Protects land-fat realms (65k+ acres with <4% temples)
- **Turn Calculation**: 170 turns at BR 16 to build sufficient temples vs instant fog

### Goblin - Kobold Rage
- **Mechanic**: T2 units gain combat bonus during middle age
- **Activation Period**: Specific age threshold (middle game)
- **Unit Affected**: Kobolds (T2 Goblin units)
- **Combat Bonus**: Significant attack value increase during rage period
- **Strategic Timing**: Coordinate wars during kobold rage window

### Elemental - Fort Destruction on Controlled Strike
- **Mechanic**: Controlled strikes destroy enemy fortifications
- **Standard CS**: Only affects troops and some structures
- **Elemental CS**: Specifically targets and destroys forts
- **Strategic Value**: Weakens defensive positions before full assault
- **Synergy**: Combines with Droben ground attacks (Demons anchor Bunar assaults)

### Centaur - Scum Killing Ability
- **Mechanic**: Can eliminate enemy scum through special operations
- **Standard Scum Combat**: Scum vs scum casualties only
- **Centaur Advantage**: Direct scum elimination capability
- **Strategic Application**: Remove enemy intelligence and theft protection
- **Limitation**: Still inferior overall due to poor war/sorcery stats

### Sidhe - Circle Summoning
- **Mechanic**: Can summon additional temples during combat
- **Standard Building**: Temples only built during peace/building phases
- **Sidhe Advantage**: Emergency temple construction during warfare
- **Application**: Massacre/Bloodbath games with higher turn rates
- **Strategic Value**: Maintain magical capability under pressure

- **Summon Capability**: 3.04% of networth in additional troops
