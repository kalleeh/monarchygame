# Thievery (Scum) Screen - Official Forum Documentation

**Source**: https://www.monarchygame.com/boards/viewtopic.php?f=116&t=1736

## Overview
Selection of black ops scum operations.

### Thief Units by Race
- **Human**: Citites
- **Elven**: Changelings
- **Goblin**: Drow
- **Droben**: Satyrs
- **Vampire**: Shakes
- **Elemental**: Aswang
- (Other races have equivalent units)

## Mechanics

### Success Requirements
- Need **more thieves than enemy** to succeed
- Large enough difference = execute objective without target knowing who was responsible
- Each race has different names for functions but appear in same order

### Turn Costs
- **Base**: 2 or more turns
- **Scaling**: Depends on how close your networth is to target's networth

## Scum Operations

### 1. Loot
**Effect**: Steal gold from enemy treasury
- Successful operation removes money from target
- Money transferred to your kingdom

### 2. Rob Guildhalls
**Effect**: Destroy enemy guildhalls
- Damages enemy economy
- Forces target to spend turns repairing damage

### 3. Spread Dissention
**Effect**: Kill enemy peasants
- Targets peasant population
- If enough peasants killed: **target kingdom destroyed**

### 4. Sabotage Alliances
**Effect**: Remove alliance support for one action
- Removes support target kingdom gains from allies
- **Duration**: One offensive or defensive action only
- Must sabotage again before next attack to maintain effect
- Alliance support returns after initial hit unless re-sabotaged

### 5. Intercept Caravans
**Effect**: Ambush money transfers
- Thieves lie in wait for caravan attempts
- **Trigger**: Next time caravan sent from kingdom
- Money diverted to your treasury
- **Note**: Does not detract from your thief count or effectiveness while waiting

### 6. Desecrate Temples
**Effect**: Destroy enemy temples
- Reduces defense against sorcery attacks
- Reduces potential for offensive sorcery damage

## Strategic Notes
- All operations pit your thieves against enemy equivalent units
- Success depends on numerical superiority
- Stealth possible with large thief advantage
- Race-specific names but identical functionality
