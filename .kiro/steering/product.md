# Product Overview - Monarchy Game Modernization

## Product Purpose
Modernize the classic Monarchy/Canon browser-based strategy game while preserving the deep strategic gameplay that made it successful for 25+ years. Create a contemporary web application that attracts both veteran players and newcomers to the turn-based kingdom management genre.

## Target Users

### Primary Users
- **Veteran Players (40%)**: Former Monarchy/Canon players seeking nostalgia with modern convenience
  - Age: 35-55
  - Expect complex strategic depth
  - Value long-term gameplay progression
  - Prefer desktop experience but want mobile access

- **Strategy Game Enthusiasts (35%)**: Players of Civilization, Europa Universalis, Total War
  - Age: 25-45  
  - Appreciate turn-based mechanics
  - Enjoy resource management and diplomacy
  - Willing to invest time in learning complex systems

- **Casual Strategy Players (25%)**: Mobile strategy game players looking for deeper gameplay
  - Age: 20-40
  - Want accessible entry point
  - Prefer shorter play sessions
  - Need clear tutorials and guidance

### Secondary Users
- **Content Creators**: Streamers and YouTubers covering strategy games
- **Gaming Communities**: Discord servers, Reddit communities focused on strategy games

## Key Features

### Core Gameplay (Must Have)
- **Kingdom Management**: Build and upgrade territories, manage resources (gold, population, military)
- **Race Selection**: Multiple fantasy races with unique bonuses/penalties and playstyles
- **Turn-Based Combat**: Strategic warfare with unit types, terrain, and tactical decisions
- **Diplomacy System**: Form alliances, trade agreements, and negotiate with other players
- **Multiple Victory Conditions**: Domination, economic, territorial conquest
- **Resource Management**: Complex economy with production, consumption, and trade

### Modern Enhancements (Should Have)
- **Real-Time Notifications**: Push notifications for attacks, diplomacy, resource completion
- **Interactive Maps**: Visual kingdom representation instead of text-based navigation
- **Guild Chat**: Real-time messaging for alliance coordination
- **Mobile Responsive**: Touch-friendly interface for on-the-go management
- **Tutorial System**: Guided onboarding for new players
- **Battle Replays**: Review past conflicts and strategic decisions

### Social Features (Could Have)
- **Player Profiles**: Statistics, achievements, historical performance
- **Spectator Mode**: Watch ongoing battles and major events
- **Leaderboards**: Rankings by various metrics
- **Community Forums**: In-game discussion boards

## Business Objectives

### Primary Goals
1. **Validate Market Demand**: Prove there's appetite for modernized classic strategy games
2. **Build Sustainable Player Base**: Achieve 500-1000 concurrent players within 6 months
3. **Demonstrate Technical Capability**: Showcase modern web development skills
4. **Create Portfolio Piece**: High-quality project for professional development

### Success Metrics
- **Player Retention**: 30% of players return after 7 days, 15% after 30 days
- **Session Length**: Average 45+ minutes per session
- **Community Engagement**: Active alliance formation and diplomatic activity
- **Technical Performance**: <2 second load times, 99.5% uptime
- **User Satisfaction**: 4.0+ rating from player feedback

### Revenue Model (Future)
- **Freemium**: Free core game with optional premium features
- **Cosmetic Purchases**: Kingdom themes, unit skins, profile customization
- **Convenience Features**: Additional kingdom slots, faster building times
- **No Pay-to-Win**: All gameplay advantages must be earnable through play

## Product Constraints

### Technical Constraints
- **AWS Amplify Gen 2 + Aurora Serverless v2**: TypeScript-first backend with PostgreSQL for complex game queries
- **Single Developer**: All development, design, and testing by one person
- **Budget**: Leverage AWS free tier, minimize operational costs
- **Timeline**: MVP within 3-4 months for initial validation

### Design Constraints
- **Authenticity**: Must preserve core mechanics that made original successful
- **Accessibility**: Support screen readers, keyboard navigation
- **Performance**: Work on mobile devices with limited bandwidth
- **Browser Support**: Modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)

### Business Constraints
- **Legal**: Ensure no copyright infringement on original game assets
- **Community**: Respect existing player community and their feedback
- **Scope**: Focus on core features first, avoid feature creep

## Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors
- **Original Monarchy**: Still running but dated interface
- **Browser Strategy Games**: Tribal Wars, Forge of Empires, Grepolis

### Indirect Competitors  
- **Mobile Strategy**: Clash of Clans, Rise of Kingdoms
- **PC Strategy**: Civilization VI, Crusader Kings III
- **Turn-Based Games**: XCOM series, Total War series

### Competitive Advantages
- **Proven Gameplay**: 25+ years of validated game mechanics
- **Niche Focus**: Dedicated to deep strategy vs. casual mobile games
- **Modern Technology**: Real-time features with classic turn-based core
- **Community Heritage**: Built for players who understand the genre

## Risk Assessment

### High Risk
- **Market Size**: Niche audience may be too small for sustainability
- **Competition**: Established games with large player bases
- **Development Complexity**: Single developer building complex multiplayer game

### Medium Risk
- **Technical Challenges**: Real-time features, game balance, scaling
- **Player Acquisition**: Reaching target audience without marketing budget
- **Feature Scope**: Balancing authenticity with modern expectations

### Low Risk
- **Technology Stack**: AWS Amplify Gen 2 is well-documented and supported
- **Core Concept**: Proven game mechanics reduce design risk
- **Development Skills**: Strong technical foundation for implementation
